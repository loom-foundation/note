# Prose conventions

These conventions are the prose choices this corpus makes where the method leaves style open; they govern the prose of the corpus's own artifacts and the methodology meta.

They do not govern vendored or external text the repository only hosts: the per-side `LICENSE` (the verbatim license text), `LICENSE.md`, `NOTICE`, `PATENTS.md`, `CONTRIBUTING.md`, and `DCO` governance files, and anything generated or git-ignored are out of scope.

The active conventions, each with its single authoritative home:

- [No sentence dashes](../loom/note/conventions/no-sentence-dashes.md){id=note:conv:9y63hs5}: no em-dash or spaced hyphen mid-sentence; compound hyphens and table-cell markers are unaffected.
- [American English spelling](../loom/note/conventions/american-english-spelling.md){id=note:conv:7fpznmg}: en-US spelling; proper names keep their source spelling.

The method itself carries the remaining structural rules, so they are not corpus choices and are not listed here: the single blank line between frontmatter and lead is fixed by [The substrate](../loom/note/specifications/note/substrate.md){id=note:spec:tkhd63e}; semantic line breaks are the substrate's recommended wrapping discipline, not a gate; and Title Case for defined Terms and Personas is fixed in [The spine pack](../loom/note/specifications/note/spine-pack.md){id=note:spec:6kjja5r}, the wrapping rationale recorded in [Semantic line breaks in artifact prose](../loom/note/decisions/semantic-line-breaks.md){id=note:dec:sw4k9n2}.

Cite by reference rather than restating (see [Single Source of Truth](../loom/note/principles/single-source-of-truth.md){id=note:principle:8691937}).
