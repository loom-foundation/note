Your team keeps the words and work products it already uses: you enable the kind packages whose kinds match your practice, map your status and verb tokens once onto the method's canonical ones, and switch on only the rigor you actually need, so adoption reads as recognition of how you already work rather than a demand to relearn a fixed set of artifacts.

Renaming the kinds themselves to arbitrary house terms is deliberately not offered in this version: the vocabulary map covers statuses and verbs, and the kind packages carry the kind names.
