The conditions that define success, written in plain words before anyone builds, as a standalone artifact you can show and sign off, kept beside the requirement and traceable to the test that later proves it.

What acceptable means is fixed up front and on the record, in the author's own register rather than buried in a check written later by someone else, so the agreement comes before the build and outlives it.
