kind: pain-reliever
status: current

The account you carry into investor meetings, board reviews, and press is read off the actual product commitments rather than reassembled from memory, so the story holds up to due diligence and customer scrutiny instead of springing a discrepancy at the worst possible moment. Because it is authored beside the commitments it rests on, a claim they can no longer back surfaces as you write it, not after the damage is done.
