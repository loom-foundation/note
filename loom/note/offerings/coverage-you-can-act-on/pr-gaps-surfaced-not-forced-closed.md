A gap is reported, not resolved: a need with no offering is left standing as an open opportunity rather than auto-closed, so you are never pushed to build an offering against a need still being discovered. Because the fit is a derived view that simply records which pains are uncovered and which relievers are orphaned ([The discovery pack](../../specifications/note/discovery-pack.md){id=note:spec:w6nsgt1}, [Offering, fit, and the value proposition](../../decisions/offering-fit-and-value-proposition.md){id=note:dec:cjqwsxt}), surfacing a gap carries no demand to clear it, and the decision to invest stays yours.
