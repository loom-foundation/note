Your unserved needs are ranked by ripeness straight from their facet status: a need whose jobs, pains, and gains are all current rises as ripe to design an offering against, and a need still carried as draft drops as still being discovered and premature to build for, so you can pick the opportunity that is actually ready rather than guessing.
