Every gap is reported with its direction read off the trace, so a need with no offering reads as an opportunity in the natural order of discovery, an offering whose pains and gains are not all relieved or created reads as an incomplete fit to finish, and a requirement or specification with no need reads as the orphaned justification it is. Because the fit between an offering and a need is derived from the `relieves` and `creates` edges rather than authored ([The discovery pack](../../specifications/note/discovery-pack.md){id=note:spec:w6nsgt1}, [Offering, fit, and the value proposition](../../decisions/offering-fit-and-value-proposition.md){id=note:dec:cjqwsxt}), the three directions fall out of which side is missing, and a healthy unserved need stops reading the same as the real smell of building with no why.
