The `altitude` field on a Principle artifact names the level at which a weighted commitment operates and the reader it serves: value (who we are), operating (how we work), or authoring (how we write the corpus).

The three altitudes are a reading and routing lens on the one Principle kind, not three kinds: every altitude is a weighted commitment balanced against others, and the field records a principle's home altitude where more than one applies.
A "company value" is therefore recorded as a principle at the `value` altitude, not as a separate kind; naming the altitude lets an actor group, filter, and route principles, a value-and-operating manifesto apart from an authoring guide, without inferring the level from prose.
