An undesired outcome the persona already endures: a negative state of affairs the persona is living with now, not merely fearing.

## Inclusion test

Is the pain something the persona is already experiencing and suffering, as opposed to something that prevents them from starting or getting the Job done, or something they merely fear might happen?
If the persona endures it now, it is a Problem; if it blocks the Job itself, it is an Obstacle; if it is potential and unrealized, it is a Risk.

## Origin

General English (Oxford: "a thing that is difficult to deal with or hard to understand; a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful").
The Value Proposition Canvas (Osterwalder et al.) lists undesired outcomes and problems as the first pain category: functional, emotional, or social outcomes the customer experiences negatively while getting a Job done.
"Problem" is the closed-vocabulary token for this sub-kind; the full description lives in the Term body, and the token collapses the canvas's "undesired outcome or problem" pairing to its second element, the shorter and equally precise word.
