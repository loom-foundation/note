Something that stands in the way of the persona starting or getting the Job done: a barrier that blocks or severely impedes the Job itself, not merely an unpleasant outcome once the Job is underway.

## Inclusion test

Does the pain prevent the persona from performing or completing the Job, rather than being an outcome the persona endures while performing it, or a risk they fear might occur?
If it blocks the Job itself, it is an Obstacle; if the persona endures it as a running negative outcome, it is a Problem; if it is potential and unrealized, it is a Risk.

## Origin

General English (Oxford: "a thing that blocks one's way or prevents or hinders progress").
The Value Proposition Canvas (Osterwalder et al.) classifies obstacles as the second pain category: things that prevent the customer from getting started, or that slow them down while trying to accomplish the Job.
