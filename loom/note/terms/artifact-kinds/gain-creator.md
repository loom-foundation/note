A Gain Creator is an offering that produces, increases, or maximizes a specific Gain.

It is a facet of an Offering, stating what the product offers, benefit-led.

## Inclusion test

Does it state what the product offers to create a gain (a Gain Creator), as opposed to the Gain itself, which the persona wants?

A Gain Creator must create a Gain the persona seeks, not restate, in positive voice, the relief a Pain Reliever already provides.
A creator whose only content is that a Pain is gone is a Pain Reliever in disguise ([Earned Substance](../../principles/earned-substance.md){id=note:principle:yecemfe}); a Reliever and a Creator are both kept only where the relief offered and the gain created genuinely differ.

## Origin

The term is from the value map of the Value Proposition Canvas (Osterwalder, Pigneur, Bernarda, and Smith, *Value Proposition Design*, 2014, CC BY-SA 3.0).
There a gain creator describes how a product or service produces outcomes and benefits the customer expects, desires, or would be surprised by.

It is the offered counterpart to a Gain on the customer profile, paired with it through the *creates* relation.
