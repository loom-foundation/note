The Note is the whole woven body of intent at a scope.
An artifact is a single unit of the note: one file, one id, one piece of the whole.
The Note layer holds the note.

## Inclusion test

Is it the scope's woven body of intent as a whole?
If so, it is the note.
A single file is an artifact, not the note.
The method (Note, capitalized as a proper name) is not the term's referent: Note names the method and the system; the note names the body of intent the method produces.

## Origin

Oxford: "a brief record of facts, topics, or thoughts, written down as an aid to memory".

The method extends the sense from a single record to the woven whole: where a note in ordinary use is one written record, the note in this method is the complete, interlinked body of intent across an entire scope, in the weaving register the manifesto and method name share.
