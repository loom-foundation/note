A claim supported indirectly, by reasoning, analogy, or evidence from adjacent cases, but not directly confirmed for this one.

## Inclusion test

Is there a reasoning chain behind it rather than direct confirmation?
If so, the claim is Inferred; if it was directly witnessed or measured it is Observed, and if it is merely believed without support it is Assumed.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Validation](validation.md){id=note:term:13esbh9}

## Origin

General English (Oxford: "infer" as "deduce or conclude from evidence and reasoning rather than from explicit statements").

In this corpus's validation tiering, Inferred sits between Observed above and Assumed below.
