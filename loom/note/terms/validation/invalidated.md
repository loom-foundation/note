A claim tested and disproven: it did not hold.

Recorded rather than deleted, so the corpus remembers what was tried and rejected.

## Inclusion test

Was the claim tested and found not to hold?
If so, it is Invalidated; if it is merely untested it is Assumed, and if it was confirmed it is Observed or Established.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Validation](validation.md){id=note:term:13esbh9}

## Origin

General English (Oxford: "invalidate" as "to make or prove (an argument, claim, or theory) unsound or erroneous").

In this corpus's validation tiering, Invalidated is the terminal tier for a claim that validation has disproven, off the assumed-to-established ladder.
