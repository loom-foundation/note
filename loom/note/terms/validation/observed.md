A claim confirmed by direct evidence: an interview, an incident, usage data, or an experiment with the actual persona or market.

Empirical but local.

## Inclusion test

Have we actually witnessed or measured this, rather than reasoned our way to it?
If so, the claim is Observed; if it rests on argument it is Inferred, and if it is settled and externally verifiable it is Established.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Validation](validation.md){id=note:term:13esbh9}

## Origin

General English (Oxford: "observe" as "notice or perceive something and register it as significant").

In this corpus's validation tiering, Observed marks a claim confirmed by direct evidence, above Inferred and below Established.
