Content exists but is not yet authoritative, and is subject to revision.

Cite it with caution and flag reliance on it, since it may change.

## Inclusion test

Does it have substance the owner has not yet confirmed?

If so, it is draft; if the body is an unauthored placeholder, that is tbd, and once the owner confirms it as the operative source, that is current.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Status](status.md){id=note:term:xcen4v2}

## Origin

The Status value-vocabulary, naming the state in which an artifact has authored substance that is not yet settled.
General English ("draft": a preliminary version of a piece of writing), aligned with docs-as-code practice where `draft` is the established term for work in progress over `wip` or an in-progress boolean.
