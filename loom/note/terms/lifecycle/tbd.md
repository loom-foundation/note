Scaffolded; substance is intentionally empty, awaiting authoring.

Do not derive content from it or cite its substance; it is structurally present but semantically empty.

## Inclusion test

Is the body a placeholder that has not been authored yet?

If so, it is tbd; once it carries authored substance the owner has not yet confirmed, that is draft.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Status](status.md){id=note:term:xcen4v2}

## Origin

The Status value-vocabulary, naming the default state of a scaffolded artifact before its body is authored; an artifact with no `status` is read as tbd.
General English (the abbreviation "tbd", to be determined), adopted over `empty`, `stub`, or `placeholder` because it reads as an explicit promise to author rather than a defect.
