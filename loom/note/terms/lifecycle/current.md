Content is authoritative; other artifacts and actors may rely on it without qualification.

## Inclusion test

Has it been reviewed and confirmed as the operative source for its subject?

If so, it is current; if it still carries unconfirmed substance, that is draft, and if it has been retired, that is obsolete.

## Relations

- *is a value of* [Status](status.md){id=note:term:xcen4v2}

## Origin

The Status value-vocabulary, naming the state in which an artifact's content is settled enough to be relied upon.
General English ("current": in operation or use at the present time), aligned with docs-as-code practice where `current` (over `published`, `final`, or `released`) marks the operative version a reader should trust.

The optional `last_reviewed` date earns its place once an artifact reaches current.
