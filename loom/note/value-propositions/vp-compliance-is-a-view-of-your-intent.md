Compliance without the second system.

## The case

You already author what your system must be and why.
Map those obligations to the frameworks you answer to, once, beside the obligations themselves, and the coverage, the gaps, and the evidence an auditor asks for all follow from your own record.
No spreadsheet to reconcile, no catalog re-keyed by hand, nothing claimed that the files cannot show.
