Mono-repo or many repos: one connected body of intent.

## The case

Organise your code however suits you: a single mono-repo, or many repositories split by ownership, team boundary, or release cadence.
Your intent still works as one connected whole, because a requirement in one repository can point at an obligation owned in another and that link resolves across the boundary instead of fragmenting there.
Each team keeps releasing on its own schedule without copying shared obligations around or letting the connection rot into a stale comment.
The point is freedom of structure without giving up a single source of truth.
