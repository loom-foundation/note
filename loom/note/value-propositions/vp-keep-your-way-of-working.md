Keep your way of working.

## The case

Your team already has its own words and methodologies, whether that is V-Model, Agile, SAFe, or terms you coined in-house.
You map them once onto a small set of canonical kinds, then switch on only the parts of the method and the level of rigor you actually need.
Adoption should feel like recognition of how you already work, not a demand to drop your vocabulary and learn a new set of terms and artifacts.
