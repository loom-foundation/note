Decide once. Revisit on purpose.

## The case

When you make a decision, record it with the trade-offs you weighed, so no one wastes effort re-investigating a question that is already settled and the reasoning stays understood and accepted.
When you later learn something better, retire the old decision and write a better one that says why it is better and why the old one was retired.
That way every change to your intent is a choice you can point to, never accidental drift.
