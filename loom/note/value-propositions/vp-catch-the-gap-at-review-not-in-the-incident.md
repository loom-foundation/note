Catch the gap at review, not in the incident.

## The case

You should meet a broken contract at review, not in an incident.
Note ties every behavior to a written intent and every check to the obligation it guards; when text moves or an implementation quietly parts from its contract, the break shows itself from the files while it is still cheap to fix.
Trust stops being a feeling about the system and becomes a property you inspect.
