See every gap, decide which ones matter.

## The case

Every gap between your needs, offerings, and commitments is surfaced, and each is shown with the direction it points.
A need with no offering is an opportunity, the healthy and natural order of discovery: you understand the persona first and build later.
An offering whose pains and gains are not all relieved or created is an incomplete fit, a coverage gap to finish.
A requirement or specification with no need is the real smell, an orphaned justification, building with no why.
The opportunities are then sorted by ripeness from artifact status: an all-current need is ripe to design an offering against, while an all-draft need is still being discovered and premature to build for.
Nothing is forced closed.
Every gap is made visible and decidable and left standing, so you decide where to invest rather than clearing a checklist the tool hands you.
