A promise your product can actually keep.

## The case

Write what you offer once, in your customer's language, beside the needs it addresses.
Product, marketing, sales, and docs all speak from that one source.
When the pitch lives next to the commitments it rests on, the match between what you promise and what you ship stops being an assumption and becomes something you can inspect.
