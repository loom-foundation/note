Intent that outlives every tool that touches it.

## The case

Tools come and go; the thinking that built your system should not go with them.
Note keeps intent in plain files you own, readable by any person and any agent, with no vendor between you and what you meant.
Adopt the next tool, and the one after, without rewriting a line of it.
