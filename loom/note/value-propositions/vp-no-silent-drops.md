Say it once. It holds everywhere.

## The case

State an obligation once, and it holds everywhere you mean it to, for as long as you mean it.
Anyone who tightens, loosens, or drops it does so explicitly, with a rationale on record.
Your policies stop being copy-pasted hopes and become a single source with auditable exceptions.
