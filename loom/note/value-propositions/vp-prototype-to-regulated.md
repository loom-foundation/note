From prototype to regulated product: one method that scales with you.

## The case

A throwaway prototype and a regulated system in production are not two different practices; they are the same work at different stakes.
Keep the touch light while you are still finding out what you are building, then turn the rigor up as the consequences grow.
Nothing you wrote at the lighter setting has to be redone; it carries straight into the system that ships.
The same files that started as a sketch become the record an auditor reads.
