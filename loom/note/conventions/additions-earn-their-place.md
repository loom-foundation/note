name: Additions earn their place
kind: convention
status: current
---

A new artifact, section, field, table, or convention is authored only where it carries distinct, load-bearing substance.
The gate is two questions: does this carry substance no other location carries, and would removing it lose information; if either answer is no, the addition is not made.

When substance migrates, the home it leaves is absorbed or retired in the same change, with a citation left where the reader still needs the trail; a restatement is never left standing.

In review the gate binds: a section that restates another location is rejected rather than edited toward difference, and ceremony that adds no substance is deleted.
