A Systems Engineer or an AI Agent who needs to know whether two terms in the corpus overlap, or whether one subsumes the other, finds no explicit record; the relationship has to be reconstructed from how the terms are used across documents, and the reconstruction may be wrong. For the AI Agent the faulty inference is silent, and the error propagates through any reasoning that rests on it.
