When an Adopter commits its corpus to a method and its format, it wants confidence that a later version of the method will never strand that corpus with no way forward.

## Source

The widely observed pain of breaking schema and API changes, deprecations without a path, and format obsolescence that strands existing data.
External to this corpus.

This need is distinct from [Intent that outlives its tooling](intent-outlives-tooling.md){id=note:need:jwvn9b7}: that one concerns surviving the tool, this one concerns surviving the method's own version changes.
