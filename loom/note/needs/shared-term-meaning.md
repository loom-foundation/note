When a Systems Engineer, an AI Agent, or a Reviewer or Auditor encounters a term that carries weight in the corpus, there is no guarantee it means exactly one thing wherever it appears, and the relationships between terms are rarely made explicit, so a word may be read differently by different people and agents and the structure connecting terms must be reconstructed from how the terms happen to be used.

## Source

The established discipline of terminology work, in which meaning is fixed not only by a definition but by a term's position in a system of concepts and their relations (ISO 704 and ISO 1087), and the single-source-of-truth cost of a fact, here a term's meaning or a relationship between terms, living in more than one place.
