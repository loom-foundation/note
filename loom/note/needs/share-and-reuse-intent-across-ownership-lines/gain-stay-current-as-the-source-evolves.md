When a drawn-in source publishes a new version, an Adopter can see what moving up to it would mean for its own corpus: which of its requirements, specifications, or code the update would invalidate, and where its coverage gains fresh gaps or sheds parts it can now drop; so the choice to take the new version is made with its consequences in view rather than blind.
