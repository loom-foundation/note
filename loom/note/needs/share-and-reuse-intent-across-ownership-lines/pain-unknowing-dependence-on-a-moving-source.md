An Adopter that depends on intent from a source outside its line of ownership without pinning a version is bound to whatever the source happens to say at any moment: the source can change underneath it with no warning, and the Adopter risks building on a target that shifts after it has committed work to it, discovering the move only when something downstream breaks.
