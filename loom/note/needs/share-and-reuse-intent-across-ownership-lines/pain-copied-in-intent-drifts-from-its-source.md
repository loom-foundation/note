Lacking a way to depend on intent from a source outside its line of ownership, an Adopter pastes it in as a copy; the copy carries no link to where it came from, so when the source is corrected or revised the pasted-in version stays frozen and silently diverges, and the Adopter cannot tell its copy now contradicts the source it was meant to reuse.
