For any part of the system, a reader can see whether the recorded intent was confirmed by whoever made the decision or reconstructed afterward from evidence, and so can calibrate how much weight to place on it: the Future Maintainer knows how far to rely on it, and the Reviewer or Auditor can weight the evidence and flag a genuine gap rather than mistaking uncertainty for absence.
