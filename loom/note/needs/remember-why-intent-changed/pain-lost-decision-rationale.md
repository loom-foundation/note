A Future Maintainer picking up work later finds what was decided but not the reasoning that produced it: the rationale was never in the record, or it lived only in the heads of people who have since moved on, leaving the record as the only thing to go on. They cannot tell whether the constraint is still valid or was ever chosen deliberately, so they proceed without that check.
