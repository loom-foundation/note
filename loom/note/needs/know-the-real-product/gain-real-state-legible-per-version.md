Reading the corpus, a reader can tell what is actually realized from what is still planned, and what each version of the product actually has, so version-dependent capabilities resolve correctly (passkey login showing as available on the version whose sensor is present and absent on the one without) and the corpus is relied on for the product's real state, not only its intentions.
