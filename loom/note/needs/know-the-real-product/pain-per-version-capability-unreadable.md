For a product with several versions, a reader cannot tell from the corpus what a given version actually has (that an earlier laptop lacks the fingerprint sensor a later one adds) nor, therefore, what that version can do, such as offering passkey login protected by biometric authentication, a capability that depends on the sensor actually being present in that version.
