When a Future Maintainer or an AI Agent opens an artifact, it wants to tell at a glance whether the content is a placeholder, a work in progress, settled and ready to rely on, or retired, so it does not build on intent that is not ready or has been withdrawn.

## Source

Documented practice in docs-as-code and in document control: status fields in static-site and documentation toolchains, and the handling of superseded or obsolete documents in quality-management document control.
