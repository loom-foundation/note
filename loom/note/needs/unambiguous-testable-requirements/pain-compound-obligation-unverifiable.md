When a statement packs several obligations together, no one can resolve it one obligation at a time: a Reviewer or Auditor or an AI Agent cannot return a separate verdict on each, and a Verifier cannot author a separate check for each, so a partially-failing requirement either passes whole or blocks the whole check as a unit.
