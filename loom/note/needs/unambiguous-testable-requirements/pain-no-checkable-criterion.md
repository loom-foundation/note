When an obligation states no condition for passing, anyone who must act on it has nothing to check against. A Reviewer or Auditor or an AI Agent deciding whether it is met must either block on the gap or quietly accept a claim no one has tested; a Verifier must construct a standard of passing themselves, and a verdict reached against a self-chosen standard can be reopened or contested after the work was accepted, on the grounds that the standard was never agreed.
