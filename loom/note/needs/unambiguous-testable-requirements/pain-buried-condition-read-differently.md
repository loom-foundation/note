When a triggering condition sits inside surrounding prose, anyone who acts on the obligation reads it as governing situations the author never meant, and nothing warns them they did: a Reviewer or Auditor or an AI Agent applies it to the wrong cases, and a Verifier authors a check whose scope is internally consistent yet covers situations the author never intended.
