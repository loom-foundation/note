Because the obligation already names its own condition for passing, anyone acting on it works from the standard the author set rather than one they had to invent: a Verifier authors the check straight from the obligation, and a Reviewer or Auditor or an AI Agent reaches the same yes-or-no verdict another party would reach on the same words.
