Anyone reading an obligation to act on it, a Reviewer or Auditor or an AI Agent, arrives at the same reading as the author and as every other reader, down to which case each condition governs, so the obligation is understood one way by everyone and a verdict is never contested on the grounds that someone read the words differently.
