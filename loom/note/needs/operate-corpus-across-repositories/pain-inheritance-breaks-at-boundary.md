A Forking or Spun-Out Team that carried a copy of the parent organization's policy into its own repository has no way of knowing when the parent updates that policy, so the two copies diverge while the team still presents its intent as aligned with the parent, and the discrepancy stays invisible until an auditor compares them by hand.
