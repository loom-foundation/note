Nothing in a repository declares which namespace it mints into, so an Organization or Platform Owner, a tool, or an agent scoped to that repository cannot tell the boundary of the identity space, cannot check that the repository mints only within it, and cannot tell whether two repositories are meant to share one identity space or keep separate ones.
