Before any implementation begins, write the condition that declares when a requirement is satisfied, as a statement that stands apart from the test procedure that will later verify it.

## Illustrative situations

1. **Product Owner fixing intent.**
   A Product Owner writes the satisfaction condition in their own words and independent of how the team will later check it, so it records what was asked for.

2. **Systems Engineer recording the condition at capture.**
   A Systems Engineer records, at requirement-capture time, the condition that declares when each requirement is satisfied, as a statement that stands apart from the test procedure that will later verify it.
