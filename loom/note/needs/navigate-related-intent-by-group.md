When a kind holds many artifacts with no grouping, related ones are indistinguishable from the rest: finding the full set means scanning everything, so working with any group costs proportional to the whole rather than the group.

## Source

Once every artifact is its own file, the single-file cluster that made related artifacts legible is gone, so the grouping must be expressed another way. Inferred from the structure the corpus already uses.
