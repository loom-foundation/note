When intent changes at any point in the work, a Future Maintainer picking it up later wants to know why it changed and what trade-offs were weighed, and a Reviewer or Auditor wants the same record, so a deliberate decision can be told from accidental drift and a settled question is not reopened.

## Source

The documented loss of decision context in long-lived systems, the problem that motivated the Architecture Decision Record practice.
