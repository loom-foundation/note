status: current

A person who turns a specification into the built thing and marks, in the implementation and in the change that ships it, the intent that work realizes. Before changing a unit, they find the obligations and design that govern it; as they build, they cite the intent they enact so the unit's reliance on it stays traceable. The making side of the work, across any sustained body of work, whether a software system, a hardware product, a process, or a service.
