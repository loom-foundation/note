A person or agent who authors the check that confirms an obligation is met, and the method by which it is made: a test, an analysis, an inspection, a demonstration, or a formal proof against a requirement, specification, or acceptance-criterion. They fix the standard of passing, independent of whoever produced the work, while dispatching the run and owning the verdict belong to the Orchestrating Authority. The checking side of the work, across any sustained body of work, whether a software system, a hardware product, a process, or a service.
