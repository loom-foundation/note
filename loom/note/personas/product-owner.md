name: Product Owner
kind: persona
status: current
---

A person accountable for what a product or service is worth to the people it serves: who records the needs in the persona's own terms, states the value offered in answer to them, and fixes, solution-free and before any build, the conditions under which the result is acceptable. They hold the discovery and acceptance side of intent across any sustained body of work, whether a software system, a hardware product, a process, or a service.
