An owner of intent that spans many teams, products, or repositories: an organization, platform, division, or compliance owner, including an engineering organization that wants its intent to outlive any particular tool. They state shared obligations once and need them to hold wherever they apply, with every exception visible, so policy is not copy-pasted into drift across the units beneath them.
