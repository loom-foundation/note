An engineer responsible for a system's requirements and specifications, and for the boundary and vocabulary that frame them: a systems engineer, a software architect, or a staff engineer who decides what must be true and commits the design that meets it. They are looking for a method built for how systems are made now, by people and AI agents working together at machine speed, where the quality of what is written bounds the quality of what is built.
