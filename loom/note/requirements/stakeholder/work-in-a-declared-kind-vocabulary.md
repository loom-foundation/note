An adopter shall be able to work in a vocabulary of kinds it declares, and that vocabulary shall be checkable from the files alone.

## Rationale

An adopter whose domain already has a working kind vocabulary does not want to translate that vocabulary into a method's fixed kind set.
The obligation is that the method allows the adopter to declare the kind packages it enables, and that the declared scope is the scope a checker validates against, so the adopter is never checked against kinds it has not enabled.
