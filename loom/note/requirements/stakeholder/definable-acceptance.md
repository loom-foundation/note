An adopter shall be able to author, per requirement or need, a solution-free condition that defines acceptance, separate from the executable check.

## Rationale

The Product Owner's primary artifact is the statement of what acceptable means, authored before build and without naming a mechanism.
An adopter cannot produce that artifact if the method collapses acceptance semantics into the verification kind; the obligation is that the method provides a distinct place to author the condition, with a distinct moment, distinct author, and distinct lifecycle.
