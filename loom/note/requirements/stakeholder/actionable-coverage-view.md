A reader shall be able to derive, from the committed artifacts alone, every coverage gap the corpus's typed graph carries (a need with no addressing obligation or offering, a pain or gain with no answering facet, a reliever or creator no requirement delivers, a current requirement no verification confirms), each readable with the direction it points and the status of the artifacts around it.
No gap shall invalidate an artifact or block an operation: the view surfaces, and the owner decides.

## Rationale

The fit and its gaps are computed from the authored edges and never authored themselves, so the view is a derivation the files already carry; this requirement makes the derivation an obligation rather than a tool courtesy.

The non-blocking half is the posture that keeps discovery honest: an unserved need or an unanswered pain is a healthy, reportable state, and a view that forced gaps closed would invite manufactured coverage.

The corpus's own readiness review derived exactly this view to drive its conformance series, which is the claim exercised on itself.
