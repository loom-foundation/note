The method shall be adoptable incrementally, so that the discipline it imposes can be right-sized to the organization, team, project, system, or application using it.
A team shall be able to adopt a minimal starting point and obtain value from it immediately, and add further rigor over time, as the system matures and the stakes rise, without restructuring or migrating the artifacts already authored.

## Source

See the Source of [Ceremony sized to the stakes](../../needs/right-sized-ceremony.md){id=note:need:p7ekr3c}.

## Rationale

One level of rigor cannot fit both a team prototyping throwaway work and a regulated production system.

Mandatory full discipline either over-burdens early-stage work, and drives teams off the method, or, relaxed for everyone, under-protects mature systems.

Right-sizing lets the same format and traceability serve the whole lifecycle, with ceremony added when it earns its keep.

The requirement states the adoption property;
the mechanism by which adoption is declared and evaluated is specification.
