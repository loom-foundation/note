A reader shall be able to determine, by inspection, the single settled meaning of any load-bearing term and its typed relationships to other terms, and shall be able to tell a defined term from an ordinary word and reach its definition.

## Source

See the Source of [Shared, specific term meaning](../../needs/shared-term-meaning.md){id=note:need:w3k8n6p}.

## Rationale

A term's meaning is fixed both by its definition and by its place among other terms, so both must be legible;
and a reader, human or agent, must be able to recognize that a word is a defined term and resolve it to its meaning.

The requirement states these as properties of the terminology.

Where terms are recorded, how relationships are typed, and how a term is referenced from another artifact are left to the system requirements and to specification.
