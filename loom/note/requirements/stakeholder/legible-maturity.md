An actor shall be able to determine an artifact's maturity, that is, whether its content can be relied on, by inspecting the artifact itself, without reading and judging the whole body.

## Source

See the Source of [Know an artifact's maturity before relying on it](../../needs/know-artifact-maturity.md){id=note:need:6pxykex}.

## Rationale

Reliance should be a deliberate choice, not an accident of not noticing that an artifact was a placeholder or had been retired.

Making maturity legible from the artifact lets a reader, and an agent acting at speed, decide whether to depend on the content before doing so.

What the maturity states are, and how a change between them is constrained, is left to the system requirement and its specification.
