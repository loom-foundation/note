A persona shall be nameable once and referenceable by name from the Needs it serves, and shall be inheritable and specializable across scopes, so that an actor is recorded once rather than restated, and a persona defined at a broader scope can be reused, specialized, or declined by an inner scope.

## Source

See the Source of [Shared persona, local where needed](../../needs/shared-persona.md){id=note:need:p9k4w3n}.

## Rationale

A persona restated in each Need drifts, and a persona that cannot be scoped cannot serve an organization whose teams share and specialize actors.

The requirement states the two properties, named-once-and-referenceable and scoped, as obligations on how personas are recorded;
whether a persona is a separate artifact or an entry in a shared place, and how scoping is expressed, is left to the system requirements and to specification.
