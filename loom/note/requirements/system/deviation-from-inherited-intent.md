Note shall ensure that an inner scope can relate to inherited intent in three defined ways: adding to it so both apply, replacing it at this scope, or removing it with no replacement.

Each shall have defined semantics and shall be recorded explicitly on the inheriting artifact.

## Rationale

These are the three deviations teams need (strengthen, replace, opt out), made explicit on the artifact rather than implied.

The operator names and their precise semantics are specification.
